# Ecosystem Section (Section 4)

## 1. Functional Goal
Visualize Trexim as the central "Hub" that connects all players in the logistics market (Operational Clients & Strategic Partners).

## 2. Visual Reference
- **Layout:** "Hub and Spoke" / "Orbit" style (from "Seamless Integrations" image).
- **Style:** Dark mode adaptation of the reference.
    - Central glowing Trexim Logo.
    - Floating "satellite" cards for partners/clients.
    - Concentric ripples/waves to show connectivity.
    - **Header:** "Ваші сервіси — частина щоденних логістичних операцій в екосистемі" (Your services — part of daily logistics operations in the ecosystem).

## 3. Content Strategy

### Central Hub
- **Icon:** Trexim Logo.
- **Label:** TREXIM Core.

### Operational Clients (Left Side / Orbit 1)
- Logistic Companies (Логістичні компанії)
- Carriers/Drivers (Перевізники / водії)
- Customers/Business (Замовники / бізнеси)

### Strategic Partners (Right Side / Orbit 2)
- State/Analytics (Держава / аналітичні інститути)
- Banks/Finance (Банки / фінанси)
- Fuel/Petrol (Паливо / АЗС)
- EDI/QES (ЕДО / КЕП)
- Insurance (Страхові)
- Tech Partners (IT/IoT/GPS)
- Legal (Юридичні компанії)

## 4. Technical Constraints
- `min-h-screen` for consistency.
- Responsive: On mobile, maybe stack them or use a simplified grid. On Desktop, the full orbital view.
