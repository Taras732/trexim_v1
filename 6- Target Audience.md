# Target Audience Section (Workflow 6)

## 1. Functional Goal
Segment users so they recognize their specific pain points and solutions. "This product is made for me."

## 2. Visual Reference
- **Style:** "Rise" app aesthetic (from user upload).
- **Theme:** Dark mode, clean cards, subtle borders, high contrast text.
- **Layout:** 3-column grid (or 2+1 / 2+2 depending on fit, but workflow suggests 3 columns).

## 3. Content Strategy (The Grid)

### Card 1: Carriers & Drivers (Перевізникам)
- **Headline:** Перевізникам
- **Benefits:**
  - Route optimization (Optimization of empty runs).
  - Fast payments & fuel programs.
  - Direct access to verified shippers.
- **Visual Idea:** A map route or a "payment received" notification UI snippet.

### Card 2: Shippers (Бізнесу)
- **Headline:** Бізнесу
- **Benefits:**
  - 24/7 Supply chain transparency.
  - Verified partners (Trexim security system).
  - Cost reduction via AI analytics.
- **Visual Idea:** A "verified partner" shield or a cost savings graph.

### Card 3: Partners (Партнерам)
- **Headline:** Партнерам (Banks, Insurance, Legal)
- **Benefits:**
  - Access to verified client pool.
  - Risk reduction via transparent docs (KEP).
  - New sales channels.
- **Visual Idea:** A document with a digital signature lock or specific bank integration icon.

## 4. Technical Constraints
- Must be `min-h-screen` to match previous sections.
- Centered content.
- Smooth transition from Value Proposition section.
